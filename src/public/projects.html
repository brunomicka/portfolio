Rent a Room

Rent a room and is a project that allows you to manage rooms within a location.

This platform gives you the flexibility to:

  • Create new rooms and manage photos and selected times when the space is available for rent.

  • Create users who will be accessing the platform, seeing within a calendar all rooms and times available for rent.

  • As an administrator, you will be able to control everything inside your platform by keeping track of rentals and an overall balance of all costs.

Do you want to learn more about this tool? Please send me an email and request a demo.

Technologies:

  • C#
  • .NET FRAMEWORK
  • ASP.NET MVC
  • Customazible bootstrap
  • Javascript
  • JQuery

    Clinic Platform

    Clinical Platform is a project that allows you to manage your clinic.

    This platform gives you the flexibility to:

    • Create new professionals and their specialization.

    • Manage each professional agenda.

    • Export multiple types of finance reports.

    • Manage costs per patient visit vs. doctors' hour's price.

    • Print customized invoices.

    • As an administrator, you have full control of permissions and menus for each type of user.

    • As an administrator, you will be able to manage payments and professional schedules.

    And many other features.
